What it actually is

A real business runs as a small org chart. The owner gives a goal to a director. The director hands the work down to specialists. The specialists report back. The owner stays out of the details.

The OS Builder replicates that structure in software. It is a hierarchy of AI agents, each scoped to one arena of your business, all running on top of Anthropic Claude and OpenAI GPT-4o. You give the top of the chain a goal. It routes the work to the right agent. It brings the result back to you when a human decision is required.

This is not a chatbot and it is not a single AI assistant. It is a structured agent network with persistent business context. The system knows your customer ICP, your pricing, your brand voice, your tools, your suppliers, and your common workflows. You never re-explain.

The agent hierarchy

Built around the arenas every business has. Yours might use different labels, but the shape is the same.

Executive Agent

Reads your goal in plain English. Decides which arena owns it. Routes the work and brings the output back to you.

Sales and GTM Agent

Owns prospecting, follow-up, proposal drafts, and pipeline tracking. Knows your offer, your ICP, and your voice.

Marketing Agent

Owns content, copy, brand voice, and distribution. Locked to your voice canon so output sounds like you, not like generic AI.

Operations Agent

Owns scheduling, onboarding, invoicing, and documentation. Connects to your CRM and project tools through Zapier and Make.com.

Finance Agent

Owns invoice tracking, expense categorization, and recurring-revenue reporting. Reads your accounting data through scoped integrations.

Hiring and HR Agent

Owns job-description drafts, candidate review summaries, and onboarding checklists. Surfaces only when you are actively hiring.

How it is built

  • Folder-based agent canon stored in plain markdown so you own the artifact and can audit every instruction
  • Persistent business context loaded by every agent: ICP, pricing, brand voice, common workflows, tools, suppliers
  • Anthropic Claude (Opus, Sonnet, Haiku) and OpenAI GPT-4o as the underlying models, picked per task
  • Zapier, Make.com, and n8n for external system integration
  • Scoped routing so each agent only sees what it needs, not your full business
  • Human gates at every irreversible action so the system never sends, spends, or signs without your approval
