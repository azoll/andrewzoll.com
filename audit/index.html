How the engagement runs

01

Discovery call (45 minutes)

Andrew walks through how your business actually runs. Quoting, scheduling, follow-up, onboarding, billing, reporting, the inbox. The goal is to find the parts of the week that repeat. The call is recorded so you can reference it later.

02

Ranked plan (45 hours)

A 2 to 3 page written document. Each line names a specific workflow, the AI tools that handle it, the estimated hours saved per week, and the order to implement. Tools named come from the live stack: Anthropic Claude, OpenAI GPT-4o, Zapier, Make.com, n8n, HubSpot, Airtable, Notion, Calendly, Twilio. Recommendations are matched to the bottleneck, not the brand.

03

Walkthrough call (30 minutes)

Andrew explains the plan, answers questions, and helps you decide what to build first. You keep the plan whether you implement with Andrew, your own team, or another vendor.

The 5-hour guarantee

If the assessment does not identify at least 5 hours per week of repeatable work that can be automated with current AI tools, you do not pay. The guarantee is unconditional. Not a partial refund. Not a retry. The math has to work or the engagement does not happen.

5 hours per week is 250 hours per year. At $75 per hour blended cost, that is $18,750 in annual time freed. The $997 assessment pays back the first workflow you actually rebuild.

Who this is built for

Owner-operated small businesses with 5 to 50 employees and a real team. Trades and contractors, professional services, agencies, e-commerce operations, real estate teams, and small financial and legal practices. The common thread is repetitive workflow, not industry.

Solo operators are a weaker fit because there is not enough process surface area for the math to work. If you are a one-person business, the assessment is probably not the right starting point.

What this is not

  • It is not a slide deck. The deliverable is an implementation list, not a strategy presentation.

  • It is not a retainer. There is no monthly fee, no scoping call before the scoping call, no upsell pressure.
  • It is not AI evangelism. If a process should not be automated, the plan will say so.
